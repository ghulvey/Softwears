Who We Are

SoftWears® is a company set on making funny and great relatable content for programmers and computer enthusiasts alike. We offer all kinds of products to really show off your passion to your coworkers and friends and make you feel closer to the world of technology than ever before.

Following the Great Corona Depression, SoftWears® Inc. was brought up from the remains of a failing video game company that was soon to go bankrupt in less than a month. When life looked bleak with nowhere to go, four brave employees held their head up and worked up from reselling fried computer parts at full price to selling the kooky things you see today.
